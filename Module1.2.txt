Here is what each element does:

- <h1> to <h6>: Headings (h1 is largest, h6 is smallest)
- <p>: Creates paragraphs
- <strong> or <b>: Makes text bold
- <em> or <i>: Makes text italic
- <u>: Underlines text
- <br>: creates a line break
- <hr>: Creates a horizontal line
